# Spain Open Datasets

Open datasets repository related to Spain, designed for SaaS platforms, web services, mobile apps, ERPs, CRMs, marketplaces, HR tools, educational software, and any digital service requiring Spanish territorial or official calendar data.

## 📦 Repository contents

This repository includes structured datasets in formats such as CSV and SQL, including:

- Autonomous communities
- Provinces
- Municipalities
- Official administrative codes
- National holidays
- Regional holidays
- Local holidays
- Official names and translations
- Territorial and administrative data

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of this project is to provide reusable, clean, and structured datasets for developers, companies, and open-source projects that need to integrate official Spanish data into their services or platforms.

Especially useful for:

- Registration forms
- Academic management systems
- Job platforms
- Business SaaS
- E-commerce
- Work calendars
- Human resources management
- Administrative automation
- Booking software
- Logistics applications
- Statistical and analytical tools

## 🌍 Languages

Whenever possible, datasets are provided in:

- Spanish
- Catalan

Some datasets may only include Spanish depending on the availability of the original official sources.

## 🏛️ Data sources

The datasets have been collected, reviewed, and normalized over time from various official Spanish public sources.

Due to the evolving nature of the project and the consolidation of multiple sources, the exact traceability of every individual record or dataset may not always be preserved.

Sources used include official organizations and publications such as:

- INE (National Statistics Institute of Spain)
- BOE (Official State Gazette)
- BOA and regional official gazettes
- Regional public institutions
- Provincial and local administrations
- Official open data portals

## 🤝 Contributions and corrections

Contributions are welcome.

If you detect:

- errors
- outdated data
- administrative changes
- incorrect holidays
- wrong official names
- or any other issue

you can:

- open an Issue
- submit a Pull Request
- request corrections or improvements

## ⚖️ License and reuse

The code, scripts, transformations, and repository structure are distributed under the GPL-3.0 license.

Included datasets come from various official public sources and may remain subject to their respective original licenses and public-sector reuse conditions.

Whenever possible, references to the original official sources are preserved.

This repository is intended to facilitate the reuse of public data in personal, educational, commercial, and open-source projects.

## ℹ️ Additional notice

If any organization, author, or entity considers that additional attribution, corrections, or reuse-related adjustments are necessary, they are welcome to open an Issue or submit a Pull Request.
